Privacy Policy

🔒 No Tracking

DevToolBox does not use any analytics, tracking cookies, or third-party scripts. We have no idea who visits our site, and that's how we like it.

📊 No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. Our site is entirely static — there are no user accounts, no forms that store data, and no server-side processing of any kind.

All interactive tools on linked pages (like SQL Formatter, JSON Formatter) process data entirely in your browser. Your content never leaves your device.

🌐 Hosting

DevToolBox is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect standard server logs for operational purposes. Please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.
