About AI Cover Generator

The AI Cover Generator creates stunning social media covers, blog headers, and thumbnails using artificial intelligence. Simply enter a title, choose a style, and let AI generate a unique background image — no design skills needed.

Features

  • 6 Unique Styles: Modern, Tech, Nature, Abstract, Minimal, Dark
  • Multiple Sizes: XHS cover, Blog OG, YouTube thumbnail
  • AI Background: Each cover gets a unique AI-generated background
  • Custom Text: Overlay your title and subtitle directly on the cover
  • Free to Use: No sign-up, no credit card, unlimited generations
